Recipe #1 Pizza

Let's do delicious pizza! It's my favourite food:)

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: enjoys of eating
Ingredients Quantity
flour 250 g
water 80 ml
yeasts 3g
salt 0,25 g
sugar 0.5 g
cheeze 100g
tomato 1
catsup 2 spoons
basil some

Step 1: Mix flour water salt sugar yeast and make the dough

Step 2: It is necessary to put ketchup, tomatoes, grated cheese, and a little basil on this flour dough

Step 3: Should it to the oven for 15 minutes
